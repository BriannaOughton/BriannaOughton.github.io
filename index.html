ABOUT ME

I am a film maker

I am a student at Howard Community College, studying Audio Visual Media Production. During my studies, I has learned video editing, audio engineering, sound design, shot composition, story development, storyboarding, film history, and industry-standard software. I have experience in programs such as Adobe Photoshop, Audition, Premier Pro, and Illustrator. As well as Ableton and Soundation. I have experience with editing clips to make scenes, supplying background noise for scenes, and composing shots for short scenes. I am working towards a career in filmmaking as a film director, editor, and producer and I am grateful to any opportunity I can get.
